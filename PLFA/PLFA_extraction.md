## Equipment needed

* sonication bath
* tube rotator
* speed-vac
	* swinging bucket cfg-evaporator?
	* freeze drier?
* SPE plate
* 1.5 ml glass vials
* 1 ml vials
* 96-well Multi-Tier Micro Plate System
	* for 1.5 ml and 1 ml vials
* Agilent Ultra 2 column
	* 25 m long × 0.2 mm internal diameter × 0.33 m film thickness
* GC-FID
	* To get PLFA concentrations
